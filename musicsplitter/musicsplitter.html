Music Splitter

While working on the Transcriber, I came across Demucs: Another AI model, which is able to sepperate the tracks of anything you give it. While originally intented to sepperate voice from background noice, I figured that this would be fun to make as well.

Here are the key features of the application.

Input/Output

I designed it to be as simple as possable: you give a file, select what you want to keep, and have it generate an output file. As simple as that.

Happy little accident

I came across Demucs because I wanted to filter the audio for the transcriber. After I implemented this, I thought it would be fun and useful to write an app that could rip apart audio tracks.

Read More

Relaying

Both of these come together in this final part. It is able to send all the data it gets to a game called 'VRChat', thought their OSC systems. This makes it so I can talk in dutch, and english text will appear in my chatbox.

Customizability

Altough this app was made for myself, A few of my friends where interested in it as well. Hence I a added a few settings to tweak (and for convinience).
