Botanical Bots

Botanical Bots is a Twinstick Shooter, where the player plays as a robot on a space ship to save it from an Unknow Threat. The robot will unlock more weapons and abillities to make the mission less difficult.

Here are some of the key features where im most proud of.

Blazing Bullets

What is a Twinstick Shooter without guns? Nothing of course! So what makes it better? More guns! So in the end we added 5 different guns, each with different shooting patterns and stats. I also implemented bullet ricochet, just so the player feels even more powerfull.

More Info

Tactical Trouble

The player will find abillities throughout the levels. While we at first only had a thorn trap, but we wanted more. So I implemented a turret to shoot enemies and a healing tree so the player could heal in the middle of a battle.
