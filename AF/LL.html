Lunar Legacy

Lunar Legacy is a rouge-lite where the player must fight monster to gain resources, and spend those in the town to become stronger.

This game has been published to itch.io! If you want to play the game, click this button below. Do play it in fullscreen, otherwise the UI won't correctly work. Info why below.

Modulair Systems

Since I knew from the start I'd be making most of the game, I wanted it to be as modulair as possbile. Since we had quite a big scope, making everything modulair was the only hope of getting even close to making everything within the time limit.

More Info

Project Info

This project was made in 8 weeks, in a team. The team consisted of 3 artists and 2 developers. 1 artist quit early on because he had a personal problems, and the other developer didn't really contribute to the project, dispite us pushing them to do something. This made it so I had to make the entire game, playtest it and fix bugs. Thankfully the playtesting part was made easier due to it being released on itch.io, so I asked friends to test the game for me. But dispite my best efforts, I ran out of time and a few bugs still remain in the game, like the UI not scaling correctly. And due to the time constraint, alot of planned features where scrapped, like more 2 more classes and worlds, more abillities, shops and an actual ending with bossfight.
