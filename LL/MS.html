Main Systems

There are quite a few different systems in the game for things like wave spawning, ability using, player movement, buying upgrades etc. At first I tried to make a lot of things modulair, but that was costing me quite a bit of time, which I didn't have a lot of anymore. So quite a few things where not as modulair anymore as I wanted. In here I'll talk about a few of them.

Here are some of the key feature.

Abilities

At first we didn't know what to do with the abillities, so they ended up being inmplemented quite late into development, which is why the code is quite hard coded. They still are in need of some tweaking, and are quite powerful. Sadly due to time constraints, I couldn't add more abilites or really tweak the ones that where implemented.

Wave Spawning

Spawning enemies is ofcourse one of the most important things, otherwise there is no game to play. I made it so you can make a "Wave" with a scriptable object, by just putting in an enemy into an array for ease of use.
