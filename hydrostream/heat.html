High Pressure

A key mechanic of the game was making an boosting mechanic. But we wanted to add a twist to it. What if we made a few seperate resources that would need to be combined to gain a speed boost?

The Heat Is On

The first resource would be called "Heat". You'd gain heat passively by just using the engine, so going forwards and backwards would add heat.

Cooling is Critcal

Heat is important for 2 reasons. You can boost with it, but too much is also dangerous. If you have too much the engine will shut off. So how do I keep it cool? In comes the second resource: Water! By using water you can cool down the engine.

Pressured

By using water to cool the engine you turn it into more steam. You will use that steam to go even faster! Managing and balancing the two of these resources is critical for a fast time.
