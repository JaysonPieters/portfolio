Hydro Stream

Hydro Stream is a racing game that is set in a Steampunk-ish city. The player must race against their own time to set a better record whilst managing your heat.

Although I did most of the coding work, here are some of the features I made.

Fast Action

Hydro Streams main goal is makeing the player feel like they are going fast. We achived this by makeing the camara zoom out when going fast, adding a stripe effect while boosting and having some camara tilt.

More Info

The heat is on

While a boosting mechanic was necessary we thought that adding something extra would spice things up a bit. Thats why we added a heat mechanic. While driving (forward or backward) the heat increases. If your heat maxes the engine will shut down for a while. To deduce heat you must boost.

More Info

Destructable Objects

We noticed that driving in the world was fun, but it felt kind of dead and static. Thats why we added destructable objects. When driving into them they would fall appart and fly way depending on your speed.
